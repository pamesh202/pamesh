The Dual Nature

I operate at the intersection of rigorous science and fluid artistry. As a physicist, I delve into the atomic lattice of Perovskites and Nanocomposites. As a poet, I explore the unquantifiable rhythms of human emotion.


Currently pursuing a Ph.D. at Chitkara University (CURIN), my work focuses on computational material science, specifically Lead Halide Perovskites and rGO-ZnO photocatalysts.

Scientific Inquiry

Halide Perovskites

First-Principles Calculations (DFT)

Investigating the structural, electronic, and optical properties of Lead Halide Perovskites ($ABX_3$) using Density Functional Theory. Exploring stability and efficiency for next-gen solar cells.

rGO–ZnO Nanocomposites

J. Ind. Eng. Chem (2025)

Synthesized rGO-ZnO nanocomposites demonstrating superior photocatalytic activity. A sustainable approach for energy and environmental remediation.

Mixed Metal Oxides

PbO/NiO/ZnO Systems

Analysis of heterojunction interfaces to enhance charge separation and extend light absorption into the visible spectrum.
